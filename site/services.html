Dissertations

A dissertation, on average, makes up between 10% and 25% of total and is an easy way to boost the final grade. As the student has plenty of time to write it, the examiners, naturally, have higher expectations from a dissertation. Work needs to show evidence of use of advanced academic sources, arguments must be developed further, and independent analysis needs to be strengthened Strong dissertations are important to our clients, which is why we give an extended free alterations period of 10 days as standard.
